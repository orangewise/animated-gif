Usage

Add the following snippet to your page: 

      <!-- freezeframe stylesheet & javascript -->
      <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://orangewise.github.io/animated-gif/freezeframe/css/freezeframe_styles.min.css">
      <script src="https://orangewise.github.io/animated-gif/freezeframe/js/freezeframe.pkgd.js"></script>
      <script src="https://orangewise.github.io/animated-gif/freezeframe/init.js"></script>

      <!-- your animated gifs -->
      <img class="freezeframe freezeframe-responsive" src="01.gif" alt="" />
      <img class="freezeframe freezeframe-responsive" src="02.gif" alt="" />

Result

Click on the gif to start the animation...
